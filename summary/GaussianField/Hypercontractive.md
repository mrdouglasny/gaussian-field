## Overview

Proves the Gross log-Sobolev inequality for the Gaussian measure via explicit moment computations. The key steps are: (1) absolute moments of the standard Gaussian via the Gamma function, (2) pointwise bound $x^2 \log(x^2/\sigma^2) \le x^4/\sigma^2 - x^2$ from $\log y \le y - 1$, (3) Gaussian moment identities $E[X^2] = \sigma^2$ and $E[X^4] = 3\sigma^4$, and (4) the full infinite-dimensional log-Sobolev inequality via reduction to the 1D case through `pairing_is_gaussian`.
